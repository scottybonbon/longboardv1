How it works

HITCH™Passive House Institute certified façade anchor — up to 94% thermal efficiency at standard 32"×48" spacing, effective R-values above R60, 1"–16" continuous insulation, up to 50% fewer clips
Link & Lock™Hidden-fastener system — two-piece battens, matching end caps, multiple orientations, across cladding, screens, beams and ceilings
Tongue & GroovePremium linear planks — 12' or 24' lengths, 1/16" base metal, on-site or prefab fabrication, performs in all seismic categories
DauntlessSuspended baffle system — open-joint aesthetic, clip-and-rail install, NRC up to 0.79, easy MEP access
